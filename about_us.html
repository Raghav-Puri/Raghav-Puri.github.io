Quality Policy

  • Quality control in construction work typically involving and ensuring compliance
  • Guidelines for random sampling of the material to insure its quality
  • Total quality control at site including training for personnel
  • Maintaining all records of quality tests done
  • Maintaining equipment involving quality control

Safety Policy

  • Site-specific planning, accountability and training
  • Identify and mitigating risks to the health and well-being of workers and the public
  • Periodic training programme for safety awareness
  • Taking corrective action on safety hazards
  • The names, positions and health and safety responsibilities of all persons at the workplace
  • Compliance of all legal and statutory requirements related to safety
  • Periodic health check-up of all workers
  • Orientation program for the new workers employed at site
